Ecuador

Ecuador is a country that is located in South America and has been a country that is divided by the line of Ecuador, which bears its name for it. It has a population of more than 18 million inhabitants. The capital of Ecuador is Quito, where the majority of its population lives. In addition, Ecuador has a very varied flora and fauna, a tropical climate and exquisite cuisine. In Ecuador there are approximately 258,314 members of the Church of Jesus-Christ of Latter-day Saints
